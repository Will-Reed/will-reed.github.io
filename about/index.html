William Reed, Professor in the Department of Government and Politics, is Assistant Provost for Academic Planning.

As a Big-10 DEO and University of Maryland ADVANCE Leadership Fellow, Reed champions diversity, equity, justice, and inclusion. Reed is recognized for his ability to navigate and elevate complex organizations and dedication to supporting students, faculty, and staff.

In addition, Reed is a scholar with numerous articles published in prestigious academic journals such as the American Political Science Review, American Journal of Political Science, and the Journal of Politics. Prominent organizations, including the National Science Foundation and the Department of Defense, have funded his research.
